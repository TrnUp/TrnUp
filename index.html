Services

Let us take care of the music.

Personalized

Work directly with your DJ to channel the right vibe for your party via in-app messaging. Choose from a huge variety of music & styles to match the vibe of your pre-game, tailgate, after-party, birthday party or any event!

Streaming Live

Your DJ is "on-air" live with your party - streaming through the app. You & your guests can provide real-time feedback on song selections & send song requests.

Now (or later works too)

Book a DJ on-demand or book in advance. All you need to do is connect your phone to the sound system/speaker of your choice. We'll do the rest.

About Us

A tech nerd & a frat star.

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Two randomly-assigned college roommates at Georgia Tech with completely different interests & groups of friends bonded over a love of music. One wanted to party on-demand, for any occasion that might pop up. The other wanted to build front-end mobile applications. Together, they realized they could build an app that connects DJ's who really know music to those that want to rock out. TrnUp was born!

  • An App is Born

    After many long nights spent de-bugging code and countless "test listening sessions" with friends & family, TrnUp was finally brought into fruition. Ready for YOU to launch & listen.

  • Connecting You to Music

    When you download the app, you can immediately get started connecting with DJ's you'll absolutely love. Our DJ's are top-of-the-line entertainers who are ready to spin songs you love - on-demand or at a scheduled time. The choice to party is in your hands!
